Oushadhi
Herbal and Supplement Recommendations
Aushadhi in Ayurveda  refers to the use of herbal remedies and other natural medicines  to prevent and treat illness. These remedies are tailored to an individual's constitution and are believed to bring balance and harmony to the body and mind. By using natural medicines, Ayurveda emphasizes the importance of addressing the root cause of an illness rather than just treating its symptoms.
Recommendation

  • Drink a cup of ginger tea (made with fresh ginger root or ginger tea bags) before meals to improve digestion
Kriya
Purification and Detoxification Practices

Kriya practices in Ayurveda aim to support the body's natural functions of breathing, digestion, and elimination.

Incorporating Kriya practices such as cleansing rituals and breathing exercises can promote the healthy functioning of channels responsible for elimination. These practices support physical, mental, and emotional balance, making them an essential aspect of Ayurvedic self-care.
